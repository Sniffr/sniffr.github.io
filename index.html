Amy's Pride Primary School

Amy's Pride Primary School is a non-profit organization located in Bugiri district, Uganda, set up to support orphaned children. The district is located at the shores of lake Victoria and is victim to high death rates due to HIV/AIDS and water related death, which has made many children orphans. It is named after Amy, a woman who had much love for such children and supported them whole heartedly, which is why we decided to name the school Amy's pride and support orphans through the provision of free basic education. 

 

Our vision:

To promote and protect children rights while providing quality education that ensures a better future for both them and the community.

 

Our mission:

Provide a family and basic free education to children in need, shaping their lives and developing their communities.

 

By choosing to be a part of our endeavor to pave a future for these children, you put a smile on their faces and hope in their hearts. We've listed the orphans in our care below. Feel free to choose those you would like to support and contact us at amysprideprimaryschool@gmail.com so we can discuss a way forward. Let us work together to change the world; one child at a time. Thank you for your support.
